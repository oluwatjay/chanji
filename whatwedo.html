What we do

Waste Collection

We are presently focused on the collection of recyclables such as PET plastics, aluminium cans, papers & old newspapers, cardboard, and cartons, tires, and glass bottles. These are transferred as raw materials in flakes/baled form to manufacturers or our clients for complete recycling into other finished products. We offer competitive prices for our collection and disposal services.

We have the ability to purchase a wide variety of recyclable materials such as PET plastics, aluminium cans, papers (cardboard, old newspapers and cartons), tires, and glass bottles.
